About the event

This is a 2-day Hackathon happening at the Ashesi University campus from 5th December 2019 to 1st December 2019. There would be cash prizes and swags for the winning teams with the the best 3 projects. The participating teams would have a number of 3 to 4 individuals each. Food and all required logistics would be provided for participants except transportation to the venue.

Each participant is free to build awesome products on provided APIs and services but must meet certain criteria, including:

  • Solutions must have a real-world use case.
  • Participants must utilize at least one of the sponsors API. (Cloudinary API). Familiarize themselves with the API but trainers will be on sight to assist them with the integration
  • Solutions will be open-sourced on GitHub.

FAQ

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?

A hackathon is a day-long invention marathon. Participants work in teams to create awesome projects from scratch, learn new skills and have some fun along the way.

WHAT'S THE MAXIMUM TEAM SIZE?

You can hack with however many people you like! However, only teams of 4 or fewer are eligible to win prizes..

WHAT DO I BRING?

This is a very hands-on and grassroots event. Bring your laptops,mobile phones & all your computing devices, to whatever inspires you but above all, your energy and your brain!

WHO OWNS MY PROJECT & IP?

The team that develops the project is the owner of the project and IP. We do encourage sharing of code with others in the community or making code open source but it is not required to participate in the event.

HOW DO I FORM OR JOIN A TEAM AND WHY?

We do encourage participants to start forming teams before the event. If you would like to start a team or are looking for a team to join our slack community. You can also propose or join a team on the event date.
